Welcome to Azul

A game of strategy and beautiful tiles.

How to Play

Objective

Score the most points by creating beautiful patterns with colored tiles on your player board.

Game Flow

The game is played in rounds, each consisting of two phases:

  1. Factory Phase: Players take turns drafting tiles. On your turn, you must take all tiles of the same color from either one of the Factories or from the Center of the table.
    • If you take from a Factory, the remaining tiles from that factory are moved to the center.
    • If you're the first player to take from the center in a round, you must also take the '1st Player' token and place it on your floor line (it costs 1 point!).
  2. Wall-tiling Phase: After all tiles have been drafted, players simultaneously move tiles from their completed pattern lines to their wall.
    • A pattern line is complete if all its spaces are filled.
    • For each completed line, one tile moves to the matching color space on that row of your wall. The rest are discarded.
    • You score points for each new tile placed on your wall based on adjacent tiles.
    • Any tiles on your floor line give you negative points.

Game End & Final Scoring

The game ends after the round in which at least one player has completed a full horizontal row of 5 tiles on their wall. Final bonus points are awarded for:

  • Each completed horizontal row (2 points).
  • Each completed vertical column (7 points).
  • Each color for which you have collected all 5 tiles (10 points).

Game Over!
